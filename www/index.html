Tabletop RPG resources


Mainly focusing here on Pathfinder (both editions) and D&D 5th Edition.

PF2e - Earn Income Calculator

(hosted on Google Sheets)

A little tool to help GMs decide what wages they can provide their hard-working players with.

PF2e - DPR Calculator

Add Weapons and create any associations of them within Turns to simulate which one would yield the maximum DPR.

D&D 5e - GWM/SS Helper

Provided your to-hit and damage, gives the maximum AC you can safely target to benefit from Great Weapon Master or Sharpshooter

Awesome VTT and mapmaking tools

  • Arkenforge : Mapmaking and VTT
  • Talespire : 3D mapmaking and VTT
  • Dungeon alchemist : 3D mapmaking (procedural generation, AWESOME)
  • Let's role : VTT
  • YAG : 3D mapmaking (AI-helped) and VTT
  • Foundry : VTT
  • Fantasy grounds : VTT
  • Dungeondraft : 2D mapmaking (AI-helped, AWESOME)
  • Wonderdraft : World mapmaking
  • Azgaar's fantasy map generator : Worldmap generation
  • Divinity : Original Sin II - Dungeon Master Mode : Technically fits as both VTT and 3D mapmaking
  • Inkarnate : World and City mapmaking
  • Dungeon painter studio : 2D mapmaking
  • One more multiverse : Video game maker, at this point.
  • 2 minute Tabletops : Art gallery for all kinds of maps, assets and tokens

D&D 5th edition - Helpful links

English
